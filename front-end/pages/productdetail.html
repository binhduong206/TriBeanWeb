About this drink

Our Coconut Coffee is a celebration of Vietnam's two greatest gifts — robust highland coffee and fresh coconut. We cold-brew our single-origin Đắk Lắk robusta overnight, then layer it over a house-made coconut milk foam that's been whipped to a cloud-like consistency.

The result is a drink that hits you with bold, slightly bitter espresso first, then melts into a tropical sweetness on the finish. It's been our #1 seller for three seasons running — and for good reason.

Ingredients

  • Single-origin Đắk Lắk robusta espresso
  • Fresh coconut milk (no preservatives)
  • Coconut cream foam
  • Condensed milk (optional, 30%+ sugar)
  • Himalayan pink salt (pinch)

Origin
